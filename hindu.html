WORKING

JOur app is a crowd controller. When installed in Vaccination centers, it will count the number of people in that room. The PIR sensors are attached in such a way that it will show only the number of people inside i.e., if someone is leaving then the counter will be decreased by 1 and vice-versa. This way if the number of people inside a center exceeds certain threshold then the app will show a red alert otherwise, it will show a yellow or green alert according to the crowd population. This indication will help people analyze which vaccination center has less crowd and safer for getting the vaccine
